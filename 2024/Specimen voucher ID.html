Specimen vouchers

Images in this gallery are reference vouchers for specimens collected in Institute for Applied Ecology study of Kincaid's lupine. Those with morphospecies names need ID.

Osmia "darktop" 

Shiny clypeal margin. T2 unpitted band ~10%, longish lower tooth, shiny spot at top of clypeus. In Discoverlife osmia female temp key, I get to inermis, montana, californica, and atrocyanea. Probably not atrocyanea - not brilliant in color.

Osmia "T2 punc" 

Small--8.5mm. T2 impunctate to near apical margin, in straight row.
 

Osmia cf. albolateralis

Osmia bright-white

Wide gena. Color is much lighter. Mesepisternal hairs light. No clypeal notch. Clypeal hairs entirely white.
