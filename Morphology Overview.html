Basic morphology

We are drawing here on two online sources: Bees of the World, and Biology and External Morphology of Bees. These will provide additional detail beyond the scope of this resource.

Back to glossary for detailed definitions

Orientation:

  • Front/back: anterior=toward the head, posterior=away from the head.
  • Up/down: dorsal=top (e.g., tergae), ventral=bottom (e.g., sternites).
  • Center/edge: medial=towards the midline, lateral =away from the middle.
  • basal=near the origin of a structure, apical=towards the origin.
  • Combinations: posterolateral, anteromedial, etc.

FYI, orientation. . . Apical=towards the end of a structure. Basal=near the origin of a structure.
